Kimchi Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Fermented kimchi
  • Kimchi juice
  • Sesame oil
  • Day old rice
  • Gochujang (red chili pepper paste)
  • Spam (or any other protein you'd like to add)
  • Optional: Green onions, cheese, egg

Directions

  1. Pour some sesame oil onto a pan. While the pan is heating up, chop up spam and kimchi into smaller pieces.
  2. Put kimchi, kimchi juice, and spam into the pan and mix as not to burn them.
  3. After about a minute, mix in the rice and gochujang. Thoroughly combine until the rice takes on an orange-red color.
  4. Flatten the rice on the pan and let it cook until the bottom and edges are crispy.
  5. Optional: Sprinkle some chopped green onion, cheese, and/or fry an egg to accompany your dish.
